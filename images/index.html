What? No Joystick?

If you haven't heard of an arcade, picked up a controller, or if you've been living under a rock for the last 40 years, you may be unfamiliar with terms like "gamer", "first person shooter" or "RPG". Video games have been part of our culture for a while and everyone living today, young or old, has heard of video games but the days of putting a quarter in a machine and moving a joystick around are long gone. The gaming industry has grown into a multi-million dollar industry and as technology has grown so has the complexity of games available to the consumer.

One can choose zombie apocolypse games or war themed games, and fantasy games or sports games. Arcade style games are no longer enough for the average consumer. Video games today pull the player into alternate worlds where they can leave their real life struggles behind and save the world or play in the superbowl. The graphics are realistic enough to play like a blockbuster movie and the storylines are as complex as any novel on the New York Times bestseller list. Today's consumer expects their $60 purchase to be top of the line and if the video game company delivers, it's the golden egg for everyone involved.

This site is here to educate you and help you learn the ropes to become a gamer! So pick your platform and get ready to lose yourself in an alternate reality!
